## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### 1. Purpose and Scope

The Parties agree to cooperate exclusively with each other (with respect to this specific
Opportunity) in preparing and submitting a proposal and, if awarded, performing the contract
resulting from the Opportunity.

### 2. Roles and Responsibilities

**2.1 Prime Contractor Responsibilities**

Prime shall:

a) Serve as the prime contractor and point of contact with the Government on the Opportunity;

b) Lead the overall proposal development, including managing the proposal schedule, review
   cycles, and final submission;

c) Ensure the proposal complies with all solicitation requirements, FAR/DFARS regulations,
   and applicable agency-specific requirements;

d) Incorporate RLTech's qualifications, past performance, and technical content into the
   proposal as agreed;

e) If awarded, execute a subcontract with RLTech consistent with Section 3 below, within
   [30] days of contract award;

f) Flow down all applicable FAR/DFARS clauses to RLTech's subcontract as required by law
   and the prime contract.

**2.2 RLTech Subcontractor Responsibilities**

RLTech shall:

a) Provide all technical content, staffing information, past performance writeups, and
   qualifications related to its agreed scope of work;

b) Assign qualified personnel to the proposal effort and, upon award, to contract performance;

c) Participate in proposal review cycles, meetings, and oral presentations as reasonably
   requested by Prime;

d) Perform the subcontract scope of work consistent with the terms of this Agreement and the
   executed subcontract;

e) Comply with all applicable flow-down clauses from the prime contract.

### 4. Exclusivity

**4.1 Limited Exclusivity.** During the term of this Agreement, with respect to this
specific Opportunity only, neither Party shall enter into a teaming arrangement with any
other entity for the same Opportunity without the prior written consent of the other Party.

**4.2 Independent Operations.** This Agreement does not restrict either Party from pursuing
other opportunities independently or with other partners, provided such pursuit does not
conflict with the Opportunity covered by this Agreement.

### 5. Confidential Information

**5.1** Each Party may disclose proprietary, technical, pricing, or business information to
the other Party in furtherance of the proposal effort ("Confidential Information"). Each
Party agrees to:

a) Hold the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence;

b) Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of pursuing and performing the
   Opportunity;

c) Limit disclosure to personnel with a need to know, who are bound by equivalent
   confidentiality obligations.

**5.2** If the Parties have executed a separate Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), the terms of
that NDA shall govern confidential information disclosures. If no separate NDA exists, the
obligations in this Section 5 shall apply.

**5.3 Proprietary Proposal Information.** RLTech's proprietary methodologies, frameworks
(including the RLTech IAM Readiness Framework™), and pricing data shall not be shared with
any third party, including the Government, without RLTech's prior written consent.

### 6. Intellectual Property

**6.1** Each Party retains all ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property (IP) brought
into the teaming arrangement.

**6.2** Any jointly developed IP arising from the proposal effort shall be owned jointly by
the Parties, with the specific terms of use to be addressed in the executed subcontract.

**6.3** IP developed solely by RLTech in performance of its subcontract scope shall remain
the property of RLTech, subject to any Government license rights required by the prime
contract (e.g., FAR 52.227-14).

### 7. Proposal Costs

**7.1** Each Party shall bear its own costs incurred in connection with proposal development,
unless otherwise agreed in writing.

**7.2** If the Opportunity is not awarded to the Prime/RLTech team, neither Party shall have
any obligation to reimburse the other for proposal preparation costs.

### 8. Good Faith Subcontract Obligation

**8.1** If the Opportunity is awarded to Prime, Prime shall negotiate and execute a
subcontract with RLTech in good faith within [30] calendar days of prime contract award,
consistent with the workshare and scope described in Section 3 and the rates established in
RLTech's then-current Rate Sheet.

**8.2** If Prime fails to execute a subcontract with RLTech consistent with this Agreement
within the timeframe above without cause, Prime shall pay RLTech a breakup fee of
**[$________]** as liquidated damages, representing a reasonable estimate of RLTech's
proposal investment costs.

**8.3** Prime shall not substitute another subcontractor for RLTech's agreed scope without
RLTech's prior written consent and without just cause (e.g., failure to obtain required
clearances, inability to provide qualified personnel, or material breach of this Agreement).

### 12. Term and Termination

**12.1 Term.** This Agreement shall be effective as of the Effective Date and shall continue
until the earliest of: (a) award of the Opportunity to another offeror; (b) cancellation of
the Opportunity by the Government; (c) mutual written agreement of the Parties to terminate;
or (d) **[18] months** from the Effective Date, whichever occurs first.

**12.2 Upon Award.** If the Opportunity is awarded to Prime, this Agreement shall be
superseded by the executed subcontract agreement between the Parties with respect to matters
covered by the subcontract.

**12.3 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for
convenience upon [15] days written notice to the other Party. Confidentiality and IP
obligations shall survive termination.

### 13. Limitation of Liability

Neither Party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, consequential,
special, or punitive damages arising out of or related to this Agreement, even if advised of
the possibility of such damages. The total liability of either Party under this Agreement
shall not exceed the actual and direct costs incurred by the non-breaching Party in connection
with the Opportunity.

### 14. No Agency or Partnership

This Agreement does not create a joint venture, partnership, employment relationship, or
agency between the Parties. Each Party is an independent contractor. Neither Party has the
authority to bind the other Party to any obligation without prior written consent.

### 15. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the **State of Colorado**, without regard to
conflict of laws principles. The Parties agree to attempt in good faith to resolve any dispute
through direct negotiation before pursuing other remedies. If negotiation fails, disputes
shall be resolved in state or federal courts located in **Arapahoe County, Colorado**.

### 16. Notices

All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email with confirmation
of receipt, or by overnight courier to the addresses listed on the first page of this Agreement.

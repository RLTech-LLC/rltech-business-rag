## Competitive Context & Rate Justification

RLTech LLC's rates are calibrated to the current federal IT professional services market:

- **Large integrator (Booz Allen, Leidos, SAIC) IAM rates:** $175–$300+/hr (fully loaded)
- **Mid-tier consulting firm IAM rates:** $150–$225/hr
- **RLTech rates:** $95–$225/hr — **15–40% below large integrators** while delivering the
  same Microsoft-certified expertise
- **Micro-purchase packages:** Scoped for fast, low-risk procurement with defined deliverables
  that contracting officers can award in days, not months

The cost advantage is structural: no large corporate overhead, no benched labor costs, no
layers of program management markup. Clients pay for expertise, not infrastructure.

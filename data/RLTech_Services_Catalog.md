## How We Work

RLTech LLC delivers IAM and cybersecurity services through three engagement models:

- **Time & Materials (T&M)** — Hourly billed engagements for ongoing work, staff augmentation,
  or tasks with undefined scope
- **Fixed-Price Packages** — Defined-scope deliverables ideal for federal micro-purchases
  (under $10,000) or simplified acquisition threshold awards
- **Retainer / Advisory** — Monthly recurring access for fractional IAM advisory, policy
  review, and ongoing technical guidance

All engagements follow the **RLTech IAM Readiness Framework™**: Assess → Secure → Govern →
Validate → Sustain.

## Service Area 1 — IAM Assessment & Advisory

### 1.1 IAM Security Assessment & Gap Analysis

A structured evaluation of the client's current identity environment against Microsoft best
practices, NIST SP 800-63, and Zero Trust principles. Produces a prioritized remediation
roadmap with risk-scored findings.

**Deliverables:**
- Current-state identity inventory (users, roles, service accounts, privileged accounts)
- Gap analysis report mapped to NIST SP 800-63 / Microsoft Secure Score
- Risk-scored findings with remediation priority (Critical / High / Medium / Low)
- Executive summary (1–2 pages, non-technical)
- Roadmap with effort/cost estimates per remediation item

**Typical Scope:** 3–5 days | **Engagement Model:** Fixed-Price or T&M
**NAICS:** 541512 | **Micro-Purchase Eligible:** Yes

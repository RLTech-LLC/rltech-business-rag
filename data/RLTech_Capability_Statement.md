# RLTech LLC — Capability Statement

---

## Company Overview

RLTech LLC is a minority-owned small business headquartered in Aurora, Colorado, specializing in
Identity and Access Management (IAM) and cybersecurity solutions built on the Microsoft security
stack. We help federal agencies, government contractors, and commercial clients harden their
identity perimeters, eliminate unauthorized access, and achieve Zero Trust compliance — quickly,
cost-effectively, and with minimal disruption to operations.

---

## Core Capabilities

- **Identity and Access Management (IAM)** — End-to-end design, implementation, and hardening
  of enterprise identity environments
- **Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD)** — Tenant configuration, user lifecycle management, and
  directory hardening
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** — Deployment, enforcement, and user onboarding across
  Microsoft 365 and hybrid environments
- **Conditional Access Policies** — Risk-based access controls, device compliance enforcement,
  and location-based restrictions
- **Privileged Identity Management (PIM)** — Just-in-time privileged access, role activation
  workflows, and access reviews
- **Zero Trust Architecture** — Identity-first security frameworks aligned to NIST SP 800-207
  and Microsoft Zero Trust principles
- **Security Baseline Assessments** — Gap analysis of current identity posture with prioritized
  remediation roadmaps
- **Identity Governance & Least-Privilege Design** — Role rationalization, entitlement reviews,
  and access certification programs
- **Microsoft 365 Security Hardening** — Secure Score improvement, tenant configuration reviews,
  and policy remediation

## Proprietary Methodology — RLTech IAM Readiness Framework™

1. **Assess** — Evaluate current identity maturity, configuration gaps, and risk exposure
2. **Secure** — Harden the identity perimeter (MFA, Conditional Access, PIM)
3. **Govern** — Apply least-privilege design and role-based access governance
4. **Validate** — Verify controls against Zero Trust principles and compliance benchmarks
5. **Sustain** — Document policies, train stakeholders, and establish ongoing access reviews
